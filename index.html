How it Works

Two sides.
One table.

Whether you need a chef or you are one, NARU makes the connection feel effortless — and the outcome exceptional.

01
🍽️

For Clients

Tell us about your occasion. We'll find you a chef who fits the brief, the table, and the moment — within 24 hours.

  • i. Post your event — cuisine, guests, date, budget.
  • ii. Receive curated chef proposals matched to your brief.
  • iii. Review profiles, menus, and reviews.
  • iv. Confirm your chef and enjoy your event.

Your craft deserves an audience. NARU puts your profile in front of the right people — from private hosts to market organisers.

  • i. Build your profile — menus, style, availability.
  • ii. Browse open briefs matched to your speciality.
  • iii. Pitch directly or let clients discover you.
  • iv. Cook, earn, and grow your reputation.
